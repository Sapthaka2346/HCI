Public Market



Council calls tenders for the Public Market two times per year.As monthly Rate tax, 10% should pay to the council and tender is valid for five years of period.In order to facilitate the trading there are buildings called "Public Market Piyawara 3 and Piyawara 4" and with enough space and also a new complex with adequate facilities.
